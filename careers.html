Care assistant job description

Forget-Me-Not Care Services is recruiting now!

We would like to welcome compassionate, reliable and trustworthy carers to our team. They would be providing our clients companionship and personal care services, to help assist our clients maintain their independence in their own home.

This position requires some travelling and so you must hold a full drivers license and have access to a car.

What we can offer you:

  • Flexible hours
  • Attractive pay rates
  • £0.45p mileage allowance to help towards petrol and running costs of your car
  • Enhanced bank holiday pay rates up to double time
  • Free induction training to Care Certificate Level
  • 30 days holiday pay pro rata
  • Free uniform and equipment
  • Employer contributory pension scheme
